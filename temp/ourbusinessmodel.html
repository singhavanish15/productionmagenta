Our Business Model .

We work with women from rural and semi-urban areas. We focus on providing financial support to those poor women who are not able to take benefits of the Banking system. Our financial support is in the form of small loans to these poor women, so that they can start or expand simple business and increase their income.

Process

Purpose of Loan

Income generating, Agriculture allied and non- farm activities

Customer Age

18-58 Years

Credit Bureau

Equifax/Highmark or others

Eligibility Criteria of Credit Bureau

Not more than 1 NBFC-MFI lending Loan outstanding not exceed 1,00,000 No overdue of any NBFC-MFI/Bank

Maximum Household Annual Income

Rural Areas – 1,00,000, Urban & semi urban Areas – 1,60,000

Loan Size

Rs. 20,000/- to Rs. 30,000/- (First Cycle) Up to 50,000/- (Second Cycle)

Interest Rate

As per RBI guideline

Loan Processing fee

1% of the loan amount + GST

Insurance fee

As per policy

Loan tenure

12 Months (Loans up to Rs. 30,000) and for above Rs. 30,000/-24 Months

Instalments Days

28 Days

Insurance coverage

Member and her nominee during loan tenure

JLG formation

5-8 members

Center Meeting Formation

Minimum 10 members

Trainings for group

3 Day CGT and 1 Day GRT, CGT stands for Compulsory Group Training and GRT stands for Group Recognition Test

Field Staff Household Visits for group

100% household verification of members

Pre-Closer

Allowed without any penalty and interest

Delayed Payment

Penality as per RBI Guidelines

Disbursements

Non cash mode and in special cases cash disbursement is allowed after prior approval of MD/CEO/ person nominated by MD

Collection Process

Centre deposit their repayment to field executive

Radius of branch operation

20 KM (for above approval will be taken from COO/MD)
