About Us

MFSPL is a member of Microfinance Institutions Network (MFIN) which is a Self-Regulatory Organization - an apex Body nominated by the RBI to oversee MFIs. The members of SROs follow a standard conduct in procedures and processes that helps in promoting ethical way of doing business and brings the confidence of investors and borrowers in the member company.
